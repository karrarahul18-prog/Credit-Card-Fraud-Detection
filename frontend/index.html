🇮🇳 Indian Fraud Patterns to Watch

  • Late night transactions (12 AM – 5 AM) are high risk
  • Sudden large amounts (UPI/card) especially on weekends
  • Fake international transactions – common in card skimming
  • Multiple OTP-based transactions in quick succession
  • Multiple ATM withdrawals within short time are suspicious
